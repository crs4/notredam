Searching objects

 

NotreDAM - Open Source Digital Asset Management - developed by CRS4 and Sardegna Ricerche (DistrICT Lab)

There are several ways of searching objects.


The simplest is by clicking on a catalogue item.


Another way is by entering a text in the Quick Search field. The search will return all objects having that string contained in the following  properties: dc:title, dc:description, dc:subject.

For example, to perform a search of all the objects containing the word “cathedral” in their metadata values, just type the word cathedral inside the Quick Search textbox:




But you can perform more complex searches. For example, suppose we want to find all the pictures of cathedrals taken during a travel.

To perform this search, we need to right click the Travel and Cathedral keywords, then click Add to Search Box. By default, it will perform an AND of the results of the selected keywords:




We can also save this search inside a Smart Folder. To do this, just click the Save button shown in the Search box. It is possible to perform this search again just by clicking the “Cathedrals and travels” Smart folder:
