Adding new digital objects

 

NotreDAM - Open Source Digital Asset Management - developed by CRS4 and Sardegna Ricerche (DistrICT Lab)

You can add new objects by selecting Object/New in the menu and then by clicking the Add File command in the Upload window:




After the upload process is finished, you’ll find the objects in your workspace:




After the upload process, you’ll see a “work in progress” icon on the just uploaded resources. That means that the application is working on the new resources, extracting metadata, thumbnails, previews, etc..

The status bar on the lower right corner of the window shows how many objects are being processed.
