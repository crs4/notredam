About

 

NotreDAM - Open Source Digital Asset Management - developed by CRS4 and Sardegna Ricerche (DistrICT Lab)

NotreDAM was jointly developed by CRS4 and the Digital Content Lab of the Sardinia DistrICT, an initiative of Sardegna Ricerche funded by the Regional Operational Programme 2000-2006 of the Region of Sardinia.
