NotreDAM

The open-source Digital Asset Management platform

 
 
 
 

  1. ‣ web-based, no client installation is required

  2. ‣ can manage video, images, audio and text

  3. ‣ supports Adobe XMP

  4. ‣ allows geo referencing and search, directly on a map


  1. ‣ multiuser, with customizable workflows and taxonomies


  1. ‣ automatically adapts resources to different distribution targets


  1. ‣ scalable, relies on a p2p network for resource storage, processing, and delivery


  1. ‣ runs on Linux/Ubuntu


  1. ‣ open source, distributed under the GPL v.3 license




 

NotreDAM is an open-source Digital Asset Management platform
for collaborative environments
